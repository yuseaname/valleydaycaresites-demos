About Us

A Trusted Name in
Early Childhood Education

Since 1999, Little Explorers Academy has been the premier choice for families in Van Nuys and the greater San Fernando Valley. Founded by early childhood education specialists, our center combines the warmth of home with the structure of a professional learning environment.

Qualified Educators

All lead teachers hold BA degrees in Early Childhood Education or related fields. Ongoing professional development is required.

Safe & Secure

24/7 camera monitoring, secure entry system, fenced outdoor play areas, and rigorous health & safety protocols.

STEAM Curriculum

Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics integrated into daily activities for holistic development.

Why Choose Us

What Sets Us Apart

Extended Hours

6:30 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. We accommodate working parents' schedules with flexible drop-off and pick-up.

Daily Reports

Daily sanitization protocols, air purifiers in every room, sick-child policy, and on-site first aid trained staff.

Multicultural
