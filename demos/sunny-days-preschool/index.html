☀️ Our Philosophy

Learning Through
Joy & Discovery

Every day at Sunny Days is filled with laughter, exploration, and growth. We believe children learn best when they're having fun — and we make sure every moment is a happy one.

Play-Based Learning

Our curriculum is built around the idea that children are natural learners. Through play, exploration, and hands-on activities, children develop the skills they need for kindergarten and beyond.

From outdoor adventures to creative art projects, every activity is designed to spark curiosity, build confidence, and create lasting memories.

Safety Is Our
Foundation

Every parent deserves to feel confident their child is in the safest, most nurturing environment possible. We exceed California licensing standards in every way.

Secure Facility

Keycard entry, fenced outdoor play areas, and 24/7 security monitoring ensure your child's safety from arrival to pickup.

Health First

Licensed pediatric nurse on staff, daily wellness checks, allergen-aware meal plans, and strict cleanliness protocols.

Licensed & Certified

California Community Care licensed. All teachers CPR/First Aid certified with background checks and ongoing training.
