## Best Practices

1. **Research first** — Find the owner's name if possible
2. **Be specific** — Reference something unique about their daycare
3. **Keep it short** — Under 150 words
4. **Clear CTA** — One action: schedule a chat
5. **Professional tone** — Friendly but not overly casual
