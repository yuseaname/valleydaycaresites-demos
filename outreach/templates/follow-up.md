# Follow-up Email Template

> Second contact — gentle reminder.

---

## Template

**Subject:** Re: Quick question about [DAYCARE_NAME]'s online presence

---

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Just wanted to make sure my last email didn't get buried in your inbox.

I know running a daycare keeps you busy — which is exactly why I handle everything for the website. You don't need to lift a finger.

The free sample offer still stands. Want me to send you a preview?

[Your Name]

---

**Unsubscribe:** Reply "STOP" to opt out.

---

## Timing

- Send 3 days after initial email
- Keep it brief — they've seen the full pitch
- Reference the original email (Re: subject line)

---

## Alternative: "Quick Question" Approach

**Subject:** One quick question

---

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Just curious — is [DAYCARE_NAME] currently looking to get more enrollments?

If so, I have a simple solution that takes about a week to implement.

[Your Name]

---

## When to Use

- No response to initial email after 3 days
- Don't send more than 2 follow-ups
- Skip if they've already unsubscribed
