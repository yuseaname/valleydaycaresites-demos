## Template

**Subject:** 5 ways daycares get more parents to call

---

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Before I get to the tips, let me say — running a daycare is hard work. I respect what you do.

I talk to a lot of daycare owners, and I've noticed the ones who get the most inquiries do these 5 things:

**1. Show your face online**
Parents want to know who's watching their kids. A simple "Meet the Director" page builds instant trust.

**2. Post photos (with permission)**
Happy kids = happy parents. A few classroom photos say more than 1000 words.

**3. Make contact EASY**
Put your phone number in BIG letters. Better yet, add a contact form that works on mobile.

**4. List your hours clearly**
Nothing frustrates parents more than not knowing when you're open.

**5. Collect testimonials**
Ask happy parents for a quick quote. Display them proudly.

---

If you're thinking "I should really get a website" — I can help with that.

The free sample offer is still on the table. Just hit reply and I'll set it up.

Best,
[Your Name]
ValleyDaycareSites.com

**Unsubscribe:** Reply "STOP" to opt out.

## Purpose

- Demonstrate expertise
- Provide genuine value
